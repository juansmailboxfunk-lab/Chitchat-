FAQs

Do you see children of all ages?

Mostly ages 2–10. If you’re unsure, message me and I’ll advise or refer.

Do you offer teletherapy?

Yes—video sessions work well with parent involvement and screen‑free activities.

How many sessions will we need?

Varies by goal. We’ll set a clear plan after the first visit and review often.

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes—sessions in English or Spanish, whatever works best for your family.
